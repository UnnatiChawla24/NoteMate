📘 NoteMate Documentation

🧠 What is NoteMate?

NoteMate is your personal AI-powered assistant designed to help you interact with PDF documents easily.

Upload any PDF, ask questions from it, get instant answers, and even listen to the summaries — all through a friendly and simple UI.

🚀 Getting Started

  1. 📂 Uploading a PDF: Use the Upload PDF option in the sidebar. Select a file and wait for confirmation.
  2. ❓ Asking Questions: Type your question in the box and click the Ask button.
  3. 📄 Summarizing: Click the Summarize button to get a short summary.
  4. 🔊 Listen: Click "Read Me" to hear summaries using text-to-speech.

🌟 Features

  • ✅ Upload PDF files (one at a time)
  • ✅ Ask questions from the PDF content
  • ✅ Generate summaries
  • ✅ Text-to-speech for answers
  • ✅ Sidebar with Help and FAQ
  • ✅ Clean and intuitive UI

💡 Tips & Best Practices

  • Use PDFs with readable, selectable text (not scanned images).
  • Ask clear and specific questions.
  • If UI doesn’t respond, refresh the page.

❓ FAQs

Q1: How do I upload a PDF?
→ Use the upload button in the sidebar.
Q2: Can I ask any question?
→ Yes, as long as it's based on the uploaded PDF.
Q3: Can I listen to answers?
→ Yes, click the Read Me button under the answer box.
Q4: Does the app work offline?
→ No, internet is required for AI processing.
Q5: Can I upload multiple PDFs at once?
→ Not currently. Only one PDF can be uploaded at a time.
Q6: Can I upload scanned PDFs?
→ No. The PDF must contain readable, selectable text.
Q7: How long can a PDF be?
→ There is no strict limit, but very large files may slow down processing.
Q8: What formats are supported?
→ Only .pdf files are supported for now.
Q9: What to do if app is unresponsive?
→ Refresh the page and re-upload your PDF.
Q10: Can I save my answers?
→ You can manually copy or use the text-to-speech to listen and transcribe.
